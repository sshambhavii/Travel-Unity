Youth & Collegiate Programs

Travel Unity Youth Advisory Council (TUYAC)

The Travel Unity Youth Advisory Council (TUYAC) consists of exceptional young people who have a proven desire to lead their communities and seek to increase their impact.

TUYAC members commit to a 10-week virtual training program (with a 90-minute session per week) followed by three months of commitment to the Travel Unity Youth Advisory Council.

Career & Technical Education (CTE) and Vocational Rehabilitation (VR) Programs

Travel Unity works with Career and Technical Education programs (CTE) in public schools and state vocational rehabilitation (VR) agencies to prove young people with opportunities for career and college exploration.

Travel Unity has worked with the New York City Department of Education (NYCDOE) for multiple years and has an MOU with the Dallas Independent School District (Dallas ISD), along with other partnerships with school districts across the United States.

Travel Unity works with Council of State Administrators of Vocational Rehabilitation (CSAVR) and members of The NET (National Employment Team) to provide career exploration opportunities to those with disabilities, including youth.

Any questions, please reach out to our Youth Programs team – youth@travelunity.com
